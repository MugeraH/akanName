About AkanNames

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.Frequently in
Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which
corresponds to the day in the week they were born. These "day names"
have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person.
This AkanNames lets you share in the Akan's people culture by using your
birthday birthday and gender to let you know your Akan Name
