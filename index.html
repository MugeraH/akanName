About AkanNames

AkanNames lets you share in the Akan's people culture by
using your birthday and gender to let you know your Akan Name .
Akan names are derived from the Ghanian culture where children are
often given their first name as a 'day name' which translates to the day
of the week they were born. These "day names" have further meanings
concerning the soul and character of the person.Get to know yours.
